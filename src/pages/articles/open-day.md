## A Wonderful Turnout

What a brilliant day we had! Over 50 visitors of all ages came to explore our allotment and learn about community gardening. From curious families to seasoned gardeners, everyone found something to enjoy.

### Highlights from the day

- **Composting demonstrations** - our expert gardeners showed visitors how to create "black gold" from garden waste
- **Plant swap** - visitors left with new plants and seeds to take home
- **Herb tea** - we served fresh herbal tea made from herbs grown right here in our garden
- **Children's corner** - young visitors got their hands dirty planting seeds and learning about how food grows

## Meet the Team

It was wonderful to see so many members volunteering throughout the day, answering questions and sharing their passion for gardening. Special thanks to:

- Sarah, who led the composting workshop
- Tom and Maria, who coordinated the plant swap
- All the members who stayed late to tidy up

## Looking Ahead

Based on the fantastic response, we're already planning our **Summer Open Day for June 2024**. Mark your calendars! We'll have even more workshops, demonstrations, and surprises.

If you have ideas for activities or would like to help organize the next open day, get in touch with the committee.
